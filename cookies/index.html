Cookie policy

Last update 2023-10-25

This Cookie Policy integrates the technical details that are contained in the corresponding banner on the website www.drop.it. We use cookies to personalize content and ads, to provide social media features and to analyze our traffic.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text strings stored on your computer when you visit certain pages on the internet. Cookies are enabled in most browsers. Below is the information necessary to change the cookie settings on your browser. Cookies are not harmful to the device. In the cookies we generate, we do not store personally identifiable information, but we use information to improve your experience on the site. For example, they are useful for identifying and resolving errors. Cookies are then divided into "session" and "persistent", the former once downloaded are then deleted when the browser is closed, the latter are stored until they expire or are deleted.

CURRENT COOKIES

Technical navigation cookies: These are cookies that guarantee normal navigation and use of the website and that allow the connection between the server and the user's browser. They are essential cookies for navigation (for example session identifiers) that allow you to use the main functions of the site and protect the connection. Without these cookies, it may not be possible to use the site normally.

Analytical Cookies: These are cookies that collect information on how the user uses the website, such as which web pages are visited most often. This site uses third-party cookies "Google Analytics", "Google Optimize", "Microsoft Clarity".

All data collected is anonymous and in aggregate form therefore not profiling. The information generated by the cookie about the user's use of the website (including the IP address) is transmitted to and stored on Google servers.

The Google Analytics cookie policy can be consulted on the Google Analytics Cookie Usage on Websites page (https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usage).

The user can disable Google Analytics cookies, either completely or by selecting some of them, by downloading an additional component on their browser (https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) for deactivating Google Analytics

Optimize uses Analytics cookies to target content variations to a user.
